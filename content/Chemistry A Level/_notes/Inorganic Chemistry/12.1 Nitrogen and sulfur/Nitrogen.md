- Nitrogen is a colourless, odourless, and tasteless gas of $\ce{M_{r}=28}$.
- It comprises 78% of the atmosphere by volume.
- It is slightly soluble in water and inert.

## Reactivity of Nitrogen
- The lack of reactivity of nitrogen gas can be explained by looking at its **intramolecular bonds**
- Intramolecular bonds are the bonds **within** a molecule

### Bonding in Nitrogen
- The electronic configuration of a nitrogen atom is $\ce{1s^2}2s^22p^3$.
- To achieve a full outer shell, it needs to gain 3 electrons.
- Two nitrogen atoms therefore form a **triple covalent bond** in which they share 3 electrons with each other.
