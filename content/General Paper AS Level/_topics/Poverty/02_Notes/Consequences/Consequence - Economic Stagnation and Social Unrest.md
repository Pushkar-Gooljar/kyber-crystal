Poverty harms society as a whole.

-   **Economic Stagnation:** A large impoverished population limits a country's tax base and its ability to fund public services, hindering growth and innovation.
-   **Social Unrest:** High poverty and inequality are strongly correlated with corruption, social discontent, and political instability. The relationship is a feedback loop with [[Cause - Conflict and Political Instability]].
