Poverty has devastating effects on health, especially for children.

-   Undernutrition is linked to 45% of deaths among children under five (WHO).
-   A child born in Sub-Saharan Africa is 14 times more likely to die before age 5 than one in a high-income country (UNICEF).
-   Poverty acts as a "risk regulator," increasing vulnerability to disease and premature death.
