Poverty is a major barrier to education.
-   Children in poverty have higher rates of absenteeism and are more likely to drop out.
-   This is particularly acute for girls in low-income countries.
-   This leads to "learning poverty," where a majority of children cannot read or understand a simple text by age 10. See [[Statistic - 70 percent of 10-year-olds in learning poverty]].
