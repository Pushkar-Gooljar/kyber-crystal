Social safety nets, such as cash transfers and food subsidies, are a powerful tool for poverty reduction. They provide direct support to vulnerable households, helping to smooth consumption and prevent families from falling deeper into poverty due to economic shocks. A key example is [[Example - Brazil's Bolsa Familia Program]].
