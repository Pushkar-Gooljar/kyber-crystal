Foreign aid, when directed toward pro-poor social sectors like health and education, can positively impact growth and poverty reduction. Debt relief initiatives like the [[Example - HIPC and MDRI Initiatives]] free up national budgets, allowing countries to reallocate funds from debt service to essential social services.
