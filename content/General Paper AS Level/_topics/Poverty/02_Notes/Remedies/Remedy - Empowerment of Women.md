Investing in women's and girls' education and economic opportunities is one of the most transformative strategies for poverty reduction. It has a ripple effect, leading to higher incomes, healthier families, reduced child mortality, and significant economic growth for entire nations. Closing gender gaps in the labour force could raise income in emerging economies by as much as 23%.
