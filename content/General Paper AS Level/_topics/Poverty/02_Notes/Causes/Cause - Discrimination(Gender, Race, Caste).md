Systemic discrimination creates barriers to opportunity and perpetuates poverty.

-   **Gender:** Women in developing countries often lack equal access to productive resources in agriculture, limiting yields and economic growth.
-   **Race/Ethnicity:** Indigenous peoples are disproportionately represented among the world's extreme poor. See [[Example - Poverty among Indigenous Canadians]].
-   **Caste:** Historical systems like India's caste system create entrenched barriers to employment and social protection for marginalized groups like Dalits.
