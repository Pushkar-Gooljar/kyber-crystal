# Cause: Economic Systems and Inequality

-   **Historical Factors:** The legacy of colonialism created global economic structures that extracted wealth from colonized nations, contributing to a cycle of poverty.
-   **Modern Factors:** Extreme wealth concentration fuels inequality.
    -   See [[Statistic - Billionaire wealth growth vs stagnant poverty]].
-   **Unfair Trade Policies:** Tariffs and trade rules can disproportionately harm Least Developed Countries (LDCs).
