# Cause: Conflict and Political Instability

Conflict is a major accelerator of poverty. It creates a vicious cycle:
1.  Poverty and inequality can be root causes of conflict.
2.  Conflict destroys infrastructure, displaces populations, and halts economic activity, which in turn deepens poverty.

Nearly half of the world's multidimensionally poor live in conflict-affected countries.
