The Heavily Indebted Poor Countries (HIPC) Initiative and the Multilateral Debt Relief Initiative (MDRI) have provided over $100 billion in debt relief to 37 countries. This has allowed recipient nations to spend approximately five times more on health, education, and social services than on debt service.
