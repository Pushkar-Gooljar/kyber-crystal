The IPL is the global benchmark for extreme poverty, set by the [[World Bank]].

-   **New Line (2024):** $3.00 per person per day, based on 2021 [[content/General Paper AS Level/_topics/Poverty/02_Notes/Concepts/Concept - Purchasing Power Parity (PPP)|PPPs]]
-   **Previous Line:** $2.15 per day, based on 2017 PPPs.
-   The upward revision reflects the rising true cost of basic needs and reclassified ~125 million people as "poor."
